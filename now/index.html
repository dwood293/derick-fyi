So, what am I up to right now?

Right now, I'm learning HTML / CSS to build this website. That's right! No wordpress, squarespace, or any other 'easy way' for me.

Why? Two reasons.

1. It's antiquated, less convenient, and less practical. You'll notice a similar theme throughout my many interests. There's a certain minimum suffering I must endure. For me, joy comes from learning, doing and creating.

The world will always try to sell you a solution - don't fall for it.

2. The internet has become an odious, vile and depressing place. The algorithms have me down, and I'm losing all interest in social media beyond my professional obligations. Furthermore, as a marketer, I'm deeply disturbed by the latest trends in web development (and other negative externalities of capitalism, which will be a future blog post). They are slow, bulky, and unecessary. HTML (with light CSS for visual clarity), on the other hand, is lightning fast, lightweight, and still the gold standard.

Did you notice how fast this site loaded?
